[ MOONBASE.COM ]

It is MoonBase toll for MSX-DOS1/2.

Current Op.mode is displayed and
OPLL Drum pcm/volume can be changed.


[ Usage ]

MOONBASE [Option]

Option: /Sn: SLOT-ID
        /Pn: OPLL Drum PCM  (n=0 to 1)
        /Vn: OPLL Drum Vol. (n=0 to 7)

Eg:	MOONBASE /P1 /S23
        MOONBASE /V3
